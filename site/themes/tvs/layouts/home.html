How It Works

Four cryptographic layers working together for verifiable elections.

1. VeilSign

Anonymous credentials via blind signatures. Prove you're eligible to vote without revealing your identity.

2. VeilForms

Client-side encryption. Your vote is encrypted before it leaves your device. Servers never see plaintext.

3. VeilChain

Merkle tree ledger. Every vote is immutably recorded with cryptographic proof of inclusion.

4. VeilProof

Zero-knowledge proofs. Prove your vote is valid without revealing your choice.
